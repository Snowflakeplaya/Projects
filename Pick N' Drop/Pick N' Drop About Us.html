About Us

Welcome to Pick N’ Drop, your premier professional cleaning service based right here in Randburg. We specialize in carpet cleaning, upholstery cleaning, and laundry services, dedicated to bringing cleanliness and freshness to your home or office.

We understand that a clean environment is a happy environment, which is why we specialize in restoring the freshness and life to your home essentials. Our team is dedicated to providing exceptional cleaning results and unmatched convenience. Our Specialized Services.We focus on the areas that make the biggest difference in your space:

  • Upholstery Cleaning: Revitalize your sofas, armchairs, and other upholstered furniture, removing deep-seated dirt and stains.
  • Carpet Cleaning: Bring your carpets back to life with a deep clean that brightens colors and removes allergens and grime.
  • Laundry Service: High-quality cleaning and care for your everyday garments and linens.

We know your time is valuable. That’s why we’ve made our service as easy as possible with our complimentary pickup and delivery service!

Simply place your order, and we will:

  • Pick up your items (upholstery, carpets, or laundry) from your Randburg location.
  • Clean them using our professional, effective methods.
  • Deliver them back to you when they are sparkling clean and ready to use.
